FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS A TREE WITH FACE?

Trees With Faces TWF! are randomly generated, non-fungible tokens, as well known as NFT. There are a total of 3,333 different TWF! and they are minted, stored and traded on the Ethereum Blockchain. Each TWF! is completely unique. Generated from a smart contract using hand drawn traits such as heads, mouths, “hair” styles, eyes, clothes and other accessories such as cool glasses and icecreams, resulting in over 500,000 potential combinations at generation time.

HOW MANY TREES WITH FACES EXIST?

3,333 Trees With Faces

ARE SOME TREES WITH FACES RARER THAN OTHERS?

Every TWF! features unique traits/attributes, and some of them are rarer than others due to the randomization of the generation of the NFTs at minting time. Moreover, some are animated and others are unique, including those coming from special partnerships such as BakerySwap, Binamon and Dogecoin. Once the collection is released, you will be able to see the traits for you Tree With Faces under “Properties” on OpenSea, and, alternatively, on a third-party tool such as Rarity Tools of which TWF! is partner.

WHAT IS TREES FOR THE FUTURE?

Trees for the Future (TREES) is a regenerative agroforestry nonprofit association that works with farming communities in semi-arid Sub-Saharan Africa to end hunger, poverty and deforestation by training farmers to regenerate their land. We commit ourselves to making our first donation to Trees for the Future after selling out the collection, and it will be the community who will take over the helm for any other future contribution.

HOW DO I PURCHASE A TREE WITH FACES?

Pre-sale took part on the 30th March 2022 and the Public Sale on the 31st March 2022.

Steps to Buy a TWF!:

  1. Download the Metamask.io extension on your browser.
  2. Purchase Ethereum ($ETH) from any exchange, such as Binance or Coinbase.
  3. Send Ethereum from this exchange to your MetaMask wallet.
  4. Open our Trees With Faces website and select the number of NFTs you wish to mint. Maximum of 5 per mint.
  5. Click the “MINT” button. A Metamask window will pop up asking for connection.
  6. Confirm the transaction and the associated fees.
  7. Once you have completed your purchase, your TWF! NFTs will appear in your wallet and on OpenSea: https://opensea.io/collection/twf-treeswithfaces

Note that once the collection is sold out, you can purchase a TWF! from our collection on the OpenSea marketplace.

WHERE CAN I SEE MY NFT AFTER MINTING?

Once minted, connect to your OpenSea account using your MetaMask wallet to view your NFTs.
